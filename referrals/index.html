Referrals

We are excited to introduce CrystalRiver Lending, LLC’s new referral program designed exclusively for you! As a valued member of our network, we want to collaborate and build strong relationships that benefit everyone involved. By partnering with us, you have the opportunity to earn a generous referral bonus for each lead you send our way, resulting in a successful closing.

How it works

  1. Send us your leads: Whenever you come across a potential client who needs funding for their real estate investment, simply send us their information using our contact us page. Well take it from there!
  2. We handle the rest: Once we receive the lead, our experienced team of professionals will promptly follow up with the client, provide exceptional service, and guide them through the entire funding process. We strive to deliver a seamless experience that exceeds their expectations.
  3. Referral bonus at closing: When the transaction successfully closes, we will express our gratitude by paying you a referral bonus. The bonus amount will be a percentage of our commission, agreed upon prior to the transaction, with a minimum of $500. Rest assured, we are committed to providing competitive referral bonuses to show our appreciation for your trust and support.

Why partner with us?

  • Competitive referral bonuses: We understand the value of your referrals, and we want to reward you accordingly. Our referral bonus structure ensures that you receive a fair compensation for your efforts.
  • Expertise and professionalism: Our team consists of experienced professionals who are well-versed in the real estate market. By partnering with us, you can be confident that your referrals will be handled with the utmost care and expertise.
  • Strong client satisfaction: We prioritize client satisfaction above all else. Our commitment to providing exceptional service means that your referrals will be in good hands, resulting in happy clients and successful transactions.
  • Streamlined communication:We believe in effective communication and collaboration and ensure you stay informed about the progress of your referrals throughout the transaction.

Join our referral program today and unlock the potential to earn extra income while supporting your clients in their real estate endeavors. Together, we can build a mutually beneficial partnership that strengthens our businesses and leads to continued success.
